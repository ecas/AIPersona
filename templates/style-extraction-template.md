## How to Use This Template

1. **Fill it in honestly.** Don't write who you want to be — write who you actually are in text. Pull from real messages, emails, and posts.

2. **Get a second opinion.** Ask a close colleague: "Does this sound like me?" They will catch blind spots you can't see.

3. **Feed it to your AI persona.** Attach this completed template as context when using your AI persona. It becomes the style reference document.

4. **Iterate.** When AI output doesn't sound right, identify which section needs updating and refine. The template is a living document.

5. **Keep it alive.** Review and update it every 6–12 months. Your voice evolves — your style guide should too.
